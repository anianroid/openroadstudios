Gauley Bhai - Joro

Nepali folk-fusion rock band Gauley Bhai deliver diaspora blues on their acclaimed debut album, coloring their sonic paintings of life in and away from their home country. They invite the audience to sway to their philosphical musings, and bask in the universal sound of music that has the ability to transcend borders.

This LP was mixed at Open Road.

Smokey the Ghost - The Human Form

The Human Form is a story about a flower. A flower of five petals: black, white, brown, yellow & olive. As the flower blooms from Smokey's dead and decaying body, the cherry orange that is the sun feeds it and gives it new life. As light becomes dark and dark becomes light, a gripping story unfurls.

This LP was recorded and mixed at Open Road.
